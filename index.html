Projects

New to Medicare

The Humana business team conducted a large scale usability test of the Humana site, and a common theme was that the New to Medicare page wasn't as decipherable as it could be. As the lead UX designer on the project it was my objective to create an alternate version of the page that explains the Medicare options available to a prospective member.

Vantage Navigation

Recent usability testing exposed weaknesses in the HumanaVantage navigation. As the lead UX designer for the product I worked to provide a short term improvement to the product's usability by redesigning the navigation and integrate Notifications, an incoming feature. I provided both large glass and small glass designs.

Expertise

I have 6 years of experience in web, the last 4 of which have been as a UX Designer at Humana. I have a background in front end development in the form of a BS in Computer Graphics Technology from Purdue University, and have worked with the web in some capacity my entire adult life. I work on both consumer and internal agent facing enterprise apps and websites on a day to day basis in my current role, and have been a part of 4 successful app launches to the iTunes & Google App stores so far!

In such a large company I've had the opportunity to work in a variety of development environments- from your basic waterfall, to "agile", to no kidding lean-agile development where I was writing and conducting usability testing every week. At the end of the day I'm commited to finding effective ways to work with the team I find myself in.
